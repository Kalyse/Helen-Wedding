The Red Baron Boat Charter

Saturday 21st February 2015

To begin the official wedding celebrations we would like to invite the wedding party to join us for a private chartered boat trip. We thought that this would be a really nice way of everyone for anyone not already acquainted to get to know eachother before we move down to Villa Kalyana and the south of the island.

We will be collected from the pick up point and transferred to the red baron for a full day cruise, sailing to Koh Phangan Island with its prestine water, beautiful beaches and bays for swimming and snorkeling.

The Day Includes:
  • Hotel Transfer
  • Lunch Buffet on board with 5 Traditional Thai dishes
  • Fresh fruits
  • Soft Drinks, bottled water, coffee, tea
  • Afternoon Canapés
  • Cocktail of the Day will be served on the way back to Koh Samui
  • Snorkeling Equipment

Alcoholic Drinks are not included, but available at the bar on board. The Bartender is on board. (If you would like a beer or cocktail, please bring some thai baht, we are assured it is very cheap!!)

You will be able to get amazing pictures from the boat so bring your camera!

For more information you can visit their website: http://www.redbaron-samui.com/

The Special Day

Monday 23rd February 2015

We will be having our Wedding at the Villa Kalyana. It will be held on the beach and sands.

Our small and intimate wedding ceremony will be held at Villa Kalyana, on the private beach of Leam Sor, Koh Samui. Our ceremony will be pastored by anatcha pacharoen, who has a small parish on koh samui.

Attire

Whilst it is a beach wedding, we could kindly request that guests still dress smart, for the wedding ceremony. The ceremony will be taking place in the afternoon after the main sun has died down. Weather in February is the optimum time of year, however, it will still be very hot.

Top tips for the guys

There is no expectation that men wear suits, the grooms party are going to be wearing waist coats.

Top tips for girls

Floral patterns and Heels….. dont forget the majority of the wedding day will be on the sand / grass. Heel nightmare!!! The bridal party will be wearing wedges!! Bear feet are also okay. The other tip is floral patterns, without sounding like bridezilla, as we have been doing lots of research for the wedding we have seen lots photographs, videos etc. Many of the guests in the images opt for floral patterns on the wedding day. Thinking tropical, pretty…… however, on the day when there is twenty different floral dresses it looks like a bit of a mish mash (google it :0 )...... not pretty!!

The Wedding Reception

The wedding reception will take place at Villa Kalyana on the lawn overlooking the beautiful Laem Sor Bay.

Following the ceremony and after the family and friends photographs, you will be invited for champayne and canapes. The wedding reception will then have a seated meal, some fire displays and we can then dance the night away.

Please let us know if you have any dietary requests within your RSVP.

How we met.

We met when we were travelling. He had his crazy blonde highlights!! He was sweet, kind and funny!!

We spent so much time talking, he was intelligent and l enjoyed his company (when he was sober!) Nick would have stayed in Melbourne forever, so I decided to take him under my wing in Melbourne and took him off with me to explore the adventures around Australia and Africa.

Nick's Version

How we met.

Helen and I first met while she was working behind the bar in the hostel.

She immediately started talking to me while serving me a drink. I will never forget how energetic and bubbly she was. I was just trying to get my drink and sit down to watch the tennis but she had plenty to say! Funnily enough she does not remember this at all! As we became closer and closer I realised that joining her on her travels would be the only way to tame her wild side, so I did.
